Questions

Frequently Asked

Common inquiries. Direct answers.

01 Which videos can be summarized?

Any YouTube video with captions (auto-generated or manual). Most videos have captions.

02 How long does it take?

Usually 3-10 seconds, depending on video length and AI provider response time.

03 What's the maximum video length I can summarize?

The limit depends on your AI provider and speaking speed. For above-average speaking speed (~170 words per minute):

  • GPT-4 (OpenAI): ~9 hours
  • Claude Sonnet (Anthropic): ~15 hours
  • Gemini Flash (Google): ~75 hours

Most YouTube videos fall well within these limits. Longer videos may take slightly more time to process.

04 Can I use this without a note-taking app?

Yes! Use the "Copy" or "Download" buttons to save summaries anywhere.

05 Which AI provider is best?

Claude (Anthropic) generally provides the best summaries, but all three work great. Try them and see what you prefer.

06 Does this work with YouTube Music or Shorts?

It works with any YouTube video that has captions, including Shorts if they have captions.

07 Can I customize the summary format?

Each export integration has customization options. Click the gear icon to access settings.

08 Is my API key safe?

Yes. Your API key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage. It's never sent anywhere except directly to your chosen AI provider.
